Terms & Conditions

All prices are in USD. Payments in other currencies will be refused. PayPal only. Payment is due at checkout. Unpaid orders will be canceled and may result in negative feedback.

Orders are processed within 1–2 business days after payment and shipped the following business day. Orders placed after 3PM CST are considered received the next business day. (Federal holidays and Sundays excluded.)

If your order doesn't show as "Paid" within 1 business day of payment, please contact me.

Minimum order: $5.

I leave feedback on all orders and appreciate yours in return. If you're unhappy, please contact me before leaving negative feedback — most issues can be resolved.

All LEGO comes from a smoke-free, pet-free environment.

"New" pieces are unused, sourced from sets or Pick-a-Brick. Minor scratches may occur from storage or shipping. "Used" pieces are clean, build-ready. I only sell pieces I'd use myself.

Pending/Updated – Order submitted or modified.
Processing/Ready – Acknowledged and invoiced.
Paid/Packed/Shipped – Payment received, order packed and shipped.
Received/Completed – Buyer received and verified order.
Cancelled – Order canceled.

All lots are double counted for accuracy. Tiny pieces (e.g., 1x1 plates) may appear sparse but are verified. If anything seems incorrect, contact me immediately for resolution.

Orders are binding contracts. Please confirm your purchase before finalizing. Cancellations may result in negative feedback.

Rehnex reserves the right to cancel any order if expectations cannot be met despite reasonable efforts. This is a last resort.

Shipping & Handling

All orders ship via USPS. I only ship to the 48 continental states — orders to Alaska or Hawaii will be canceled and may result in negative feedback.

I primarily use bubble mailers, doubling up for larger orders or switching to a box if items exceed mailer size. Parts are individually bagged for safe transit and easy sorting.

Shipping is priced at the average label cost per weight category — no hidden fees, no profit on shipping. Discounts scale with order value:

  • 🏅 $25+ — 25% off shipping
  • 🥉 $50+ — 50% off shipping
  • 🥈 $75+ — 75% off shipping
  • 🥇 $100+ — free shipping

I don't ship orders exceeding 10 lbs.
